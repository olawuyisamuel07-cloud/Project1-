PROJECT NAME:- Circiut Analyzer

DESCRIPTION:- This project analize cicuits to prevent high/low voltage or current which can damage
electrical appliances or even cause fire(High voltage).it  it uses kichhoff's voltage law to calculate voltage
in the circuit and visualizes the results for better understanding.The proggramming language used was python

Intoduction:- 
This project was born out of a real challenge I faced at my university, where unstable electricity supply over a period
of weeks led to repeated damage to my electronic devices . Despite using protective equipment like a TV guard, I still experienced
failures, especially with chargers, which became frustrating and costly. Driven by curiosity from my 200-level Applied Electricity
course, I began to question why this kept happening. I researched the underlying causes of such electrical issues using some IEEE magazines
and discovered key principles behind circuit behavior and power instability.This understanding inspired me to build my own Circuit Analyzer a
tool designed to help analyze and better understand circuit performance, with the goal of reducing damage caused by unstable electrical
conditions
